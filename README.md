# Arcana

> *"Someone named Kaliastrus imprisoned his legacy in a cursed diary.*
> *If you open the book, you choose to deal with whatever comes after."*

---

Something stirs in the Plains.

A circle drawn in stone. A chest no one placed. A book with your name already written inside.

**Arcana** is a narrative mod for Minecraft 1.21.1 — part mystery, part ritual, part reckoning. The world will change the moment you arrive. What you do with what you find is up to you.

Read carefully. The diary knows more than it lets on.

---

## What Awaits

- A **bound diary**, written by someone who expected you
- **Dreams** that know where you've been — and where you haven't gone yet
- **Rituals** scattered across the world, each with a price
- A **boss** that speaks before it attacks, and laughs when you think you're ready

*The rest you'll have to discover yourself.*
